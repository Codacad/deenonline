Urdu & Arabi Language

This course is for those who want to learn to read Urdu and Arabic books, Quran Tafseer and the books of Hadith and other Islmic books, and also for the people who don't know how to read Urdu and Arabi so that they can read and understand the Urdu and Arabic books easily.

Quran Hifz (Momorization)

We also have the educated and skilled Qaris to teach Quran Hifz (Quran Memorization). You can join Quran Hifz classes with our expert Qaris.

Rasulullah (sallallahu ‘alayhi wa sallam) is reported to have said:

”A person who recites the Quran and has memorised it, will be with noble scribes (Angels/Prophets, in Jannah)”

(Sahih Bukhari, Hadith: 4937)

Quran Tafseer (Explanation)

This session is prepared for the people who want to listen Quran Tafseer and want to read Quran by understanding its meaning so that they can understand the rulings of Quran and live according to Quran-o-Hadith

Anjuman Programs

Deen Online also teaches the people who want to learn to give speech and singing Naats. We also teach our students learn to speak on different topics like Namaz, Zakat, Roza, Hajj, Sirat-un-Nabi, criticism on Islam and unpiousness among the Muslim Community.

Deeniyat

Deeniyat course is specifically made for children to help understand the basic principles of Islam such as Namaz. Roza, Kalime, Duas, and Hadith-o-Sunnah. We do our best to bring children under the shade of Quran-o-Hadith by Allah's concent.

Our Mission

Deen online is a well-organized Islamic school which aim is to provide Islamic education to each Muslim around the world so that each Muslim may have the sufficient knowledge of Quran and Hadith, walk on the righteous and straight path and live according to Quran and Sunna. Deen Online provides different courses such as, Nurani Qaida, Quran Nazra, Quran Hifz, Quran Tafseer, Urdu & Arabi Languages, basic Islamic knowledge, history of Islam and more.

Our motive is to take the young people into the Islmic Atmosphare where they can understand, inspire and guide the community, create the atmosphare the of Deen-o-Islam and make a better life according to saying to Allah Subhanahu Wa Ta'ala.
